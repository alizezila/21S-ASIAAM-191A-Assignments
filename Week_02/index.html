Alize Magaña Map Portfolio

Introduction

Hi all! My name is Alize (pronounced Uh-leeze) Magaña and I'm a first-gen student in my last year and last quarter at UCLA! The aim of this portfolio is to display my projects from my fairly new mapping career, so please bear with me! I am so excited to learn more about the intersections of GIS and civil society and I am hoping to use my skills as a mapper and advocate to better work for and with marginalized, underrepresented communities.

If you'd like to get to know me better, head to About Me :)

About Me

I love music but I especially love going to experience live music! I've been to a couple different concert venues across California, though most of them are in LA.
I can't wait for the day I get to dance and sing and yell at a concert again. Try to guess which one I crowd-surfed at!
